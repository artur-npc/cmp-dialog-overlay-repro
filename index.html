CMP <dialog> top-layer overlay demo

CMP in dialogMode + a competing native modal <dialog>.

1 · Reproduce the overlay

Wait for the consent dialog to appear, then click Open competing modal. Without the fix the competing dialog (red) renders on top of the CMP — that is the issue.

2 · Proposed fix

While the CMP is open, every other dialog's showModal() is redirected to non-modal show(), so the CMP keeps the top layer. With the fix on, the competing modal can no longer cover the dialog.
⚠️ Trade-off: this downgrades all other modal dialogs on the page while the CMP is open.
